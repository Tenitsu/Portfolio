About Project

Denebola the Leo was a vector illustration I worked on overnight. The concept was to take an astrological sign and put a different perspective to it. The first thing that popped into my head was the proverb “Wake not a sleeping lion”. Most Leo illustrations like to draw active lions and thus I took that for a spin and drew a sleeping one instead.
